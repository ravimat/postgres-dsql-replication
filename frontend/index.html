ℹ️ How Load Testing Works

What it does:

  • Creates sample_* prefixed tables (e.g., sample_orders, sample_customers) on your source PostgreSQL
  • Creates a dedicated replication slot (sample_cdc_slot) — completely separate from your production CDC slot
  • Generates synthetic order data (customers, products, orders, payments, shipments) at your configured throughput
  • Drops the test slot automatically when the load test finishes — no WAL accumulation

Limitations & Safety:

  • ✅ Automatically creates sample_* tables on both source PG and target DSQL
  • ✅ Never reads or modifies your Table Mapping rules
  • ✅ Never touches your existing tables or production replication slot
  • ✅ Uses its own publication — zero interference with your live CDC pipeline
